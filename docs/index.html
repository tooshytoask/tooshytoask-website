How Too Shy To Ask Work?

Too Shy To Aks is an interactive questionnaire for couples. We present a list of questions to both partners separately and have them indicate their level of interest. After the couple has finished the survey, we compare the answers. If you both gave a positive response to an activity, then we'll share it; if not, we don’t. That way if you don't want to get married or to have a threesome, they won't know.
